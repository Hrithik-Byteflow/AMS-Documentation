User Stories --- Community Admin: User Onboarding & Resident Oversight
====================================================================

* * * * *

US-USR-REG-001 --- Registration Details
-------------------------------------

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-01** As a Community Admin, I want to view all incoming registration submissions in a single table, so that I can track and manage all applicants from one place.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-02** As a Community Admin, I want to filter submissions by Pending status, so that I can focus only on registrations that require my immediate review and action.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-03** As a Community Admin, I want to filter submissions by History, so that I can refer back to previously reviewed registrations for audit and reference purposes.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-04** As a Community Admin, I want to open a review pop-up for each pending submission, so that I can examine the applicant's details before making an approval decision.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-05** As a Community Admin, I want to edit the Flat/Unit field in the review pop-up, so that I can correct any unit entry errors made by the applicant before approving.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-06** As a Community Admin, I want to assign a User Type to each submission before approving, so that the system correctly determines the access level and approval chain for that user.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-07** As a Community Admin, I want the system to prevent me from approving a submission without a User Type selected, so that no user is onboarded with an undefined role.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-08** As a Community Admin, I want to approve a registration submission, so that the applicant can proceed with accessing the application based on their assigned User Type.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-09** As a Community Admin, I want to reject a registration submission, so that ineligible or unverified applicants are denied access to the community application.

* * * * *

**US-REG-001-10** As a Community Admin, I want approved and rejected submissions to move to the History tab automatically, so that the Pending queue remains focused on unactioned items only.

* * * * *

US-USR-REG-002 --- Tenant Lease Approval (Owner Side)
---------------------------------------------------

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-01** As an Owner, I want to receive a tenant approval request on my Dashboard after the Community Admin approves a Tenant registration, so that I am notified and can act on it promptly.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-02** As an Owner, I want to view the Tenant's Name, Mobile No, and Flat/Unit in the approval pop-up, so that I can confirm the tenant details before accepting.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-03** As an Owner, I want to enter a Lease Start Date and Lease End Date when accepting a tenant, so that the tenancy period is formally recorded in the system.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-04** As an Owner, I want the system to prevent me from entering a Lease Start Date in the past, so that all tenancy records reflect valid and current agreements.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-05** As an Owner, I want the system to enforce that the Lease End Date is always after the Lease Start Date, so that lease periods are logically consistent and cannot be misconfigured.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-06** As an Owner, I want the Accept button to be actionable only after both lease dates are entered, so that no tenancy is confirmed without a defined lease period.

* * * * *

**US-REG-002-07** As an Owner, I want the Tenant's status to become Active in the Resident Log immediately after I accept, so that the tenancy is reflected in the system without delay.

* * * * *

US-USR-RES-001 --- Resident Log
-----------------------------

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-01** As a Community Admin, I want to view all approved residents in a single table, so that I have a complete and current picture of who is residing in the community.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-02** As a Community Admin, I want to filter residents by Active status, so that I can quickly see all currently residing members of the community.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-03** As a Community Admin, I want to filter residents by Archive status, so that I can view past residents without them cluttering the active view.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-04** As a Community Admin, I want to open an edit pop-up for any resident record, so that I can update their status when their residency situation changes.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-05** As a Community Admin, I want only the Status field to be editable in the resident edit pop-up, so that core resident details cannot be accidentally or unauthorisedly modified from this screen.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-06** As a Community Admin, I want to set a resident's status to Archived, so that residents who are no longer active are moved out of the active view while their record is retained.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-07** As a Community Admin, I want to restore an Archived resident back to Active, so that returning residents can be reactivated without needing to go through the full registration process again.

* * * * *

**US-RES-001-08** As a Community Admin, I want status changes to reflect immediately in the respective toggle view, so that the resident list is always accurate and up to date without requiring a manual refresh.
